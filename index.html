Projects

Lambda | a physics aid | λ

Lambda is a project inspired by my wife. As a high school AP Physics Teacher, one of her biggest problems was students losing their formula sheets.

Lambda seeks to solve that problem. Built on React.js with ES6 on the Front-End and using a Parse database for the Back-End to store formulas and variables, students are able to search for relevent class information and have it accessible on their phones, tablets or laptops.

What I've built with Lambda is a summation of all of the skills that I acquired during my time at The Iron Yard.
